Bromsgrove, United Kingdom 

     "Ooh - thats nice!!"
        Reviewed 23 December 2019

      
Monaco is surprisingly small. 
      We got there easily by a 1 hour train journey from Cannes. 
      The station is a vast, clean subterranean complex with two or three ways out.
      Take the route towards the 'Jardin' and you should find yourself near to the Royal Palace,
      situated on a high point this provides the best vantage point to orientate yourself. 
      The square outside the Palace is beautiful and you can explore the old town from here. 
      A visit to the 11 rooms in the Palace is worth it for 9 euros and 
      you get to into the Napoleonic relics museum on the same ticket. 
      Go then to the Cathedral and then it is a short walk to the Bus Stop for the tour train.
