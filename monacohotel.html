  The Fairmont Monte Carlo is a unique four-star luxury convention resort.
  Located in the heart of the Principality of Monaco between Mediterranean Sea and legendary Casino of Monte-Carlo. 
  It offers a unique view of the blue horizon, the famous Casino and one of the best views on the Formula 1 circuit. 
  Spacious, comfortable with private terraces, its 596 rooms and suites decline perfectly the Riviera style. 
  Fine dining at Nobu Fairmont Monte Carlo, mixing traditional Japanese dishes and South American spices and aromas. 
  Delicious and local Mediterranean cuisine at the Horizon-Deck, Restaurant & Champagne Bar, with breathtaking sea views.
  And non-stop service in a relaxing atmosphere in front of the Mediterranean Sea at the Lobby Lounge, restaurant & bar, open daily. 
  Not to be missed, Spa bathed natural light, modern and refurbished FMC Fitness Monte Carlo and pool open all year long.
